All About Me

As an upcoming graduate from CUNY Hunter College, with a BA in Media Studies and a minor in Political Science, I'm looking to break into the tech and media industry — all gas, no breaks. In college, Media Studies entailed working with front-end web development languages and creative programs like Photoshop and Illustrator. Over time, I picked up things like Figma by myself and realized that how much I enjoy web design as much as development and graphic design. Altogether, those three form a holy trinity of interests for me. So now that I'm finished up with my time in school, I want to apply myself in the workplace.


Aside from changing over my VSCode theme every few days and looking at cool posters for inspiration, I spend a lot of my time learning languages. I speak Spanish natively and learned German in university, which gave me the great opportunity to study for a month in Fulda, Germany. My knowledge on languages becomes pretty hodgepodge after those metrics — I can read and write Arabic, read Cyirllic, and get by pretty decently in French.


And if I'm not doing any of that, you'll certainly catch me zipping around the city on my bike. Don't worry about saying hello to me if you do see me, because the Eurobeat in my headphones gets really loud. Feel free to say hello to me from here though!
